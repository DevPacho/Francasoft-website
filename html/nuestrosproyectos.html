Volunteer Fire Department of Corregimiento of Rozo

This project was directed to the Volunteer Fire Department of the Corregimiento of Rozo, with the objective of supporting and improving the order of the entity through the systematization of its inventory, in addition to generating registration reports of emergencies that arise, thus improving and facilitating access to local information required by the institution.

Problem

At the time, in the Rozo Volunteer Fire Department, the control of emergencies was carried out through a national platform, in which, each time a particular emergency was attended, upon arrival at the institution after having provided assistance to the situation, all the general information of the mishap had to be registered in the aforementioned platform. This generated certain difficulties since the organization needed to keep a record of all the emergencies presented at the community level.

In addition, the entity had the need to organize its entire inventory system, so our productive implementation was divided into 2 modules. For this second case, the software was implemented with the option of structuring the assets required for the complete development of the fire department's work.

Approach

How to implement an application for the registration of local emergencies and inventory in the Volunteer Fire Department of the Corregimiento de Rozo that contributes to the order of the information in a systematized way of the entity?

Purpose

Implement an inventory and emergency registry system in the Rozo Volunteer Fire Department to support organizational deficiencies and facilitate access to information locally.
